The Big Five! A term often used to denote 5 of Africa's most famous and dangerous animals. A handful of countries in Africa have them all, including South Africa.

While there are many other famous and dangerous animals in South Africa, the Big Five are the Elephant, Lion, Buffalo, Rhinoceros, and Leopard.

South Africa is home to some of Earth's most impressive creatures, including the largest, tallest and fastest animals on the planet (both overall and land-based).

They also have penguins. Wait, what?! Yeah, you read that right - penguins!
